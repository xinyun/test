#define IIR_INT_ID_M   BIT_MASK(32, 3, 1)

Interrupt identification. UnIER[3:1] identifies an interrupt corresponding to the UARTn Rx or TX FIFO. All other combinations of UnIER[3:1] not listed below are reserved (000,100,101,111). 011 1 - Receive Line Status (RLS). 010 2a - Receive Data Available (RDA). 110 2b - Character Time-out Indicator (CTI). 001 3 - THRE Interrupt

#define IIR_INT_STAT   BIT_32_0
